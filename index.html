Modules You Need

Our goal with our PrestaShop modules is simple. Most of them are needed to run your shop effectively. We have developed modules such as our CSS Editor which will allow you to add custom CSS edits to your site withour fear of them being lost with an upgrade. Or our htaccess Editor which will let you create custom entries in your htaccess file. This is great for adding redirects or other optimizations directly from the back office.

All of our modules are kept up to date and working with PrestaShop version 1.5, 1.6, and the soon to be PrestaShop 1.7.
